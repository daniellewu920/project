How To Get Started on Orchestrating

Orchestration shouldn't be hard no? It's simply putting a bunch
of notes on a page. Wrong. Orchestration is all about timbre.
It's all about how you use the instruments and how you make them
work together to create a brand new sound. Anyone can orchestrate
with or without training. Training would just help one learn more
about instruments and how that would help.
